Ukrainian Hackers destroy Russian Drone Maker's Network

19th July 2025

According to reports on Telegram channels, the Russian Gaskar Integration plane, one of the largest suppliers of drones for the Army has been taken-out by Ukranian hackers alongside the military intelligence agency, Black Owl.

The Telegram post states they ""carried out large-scale work to capture the entire network and server infrastructure of Gaskar Group, collect valuable information about the UAVs being produced and prospective, and then destroy the information and disable this infrastructure".

According to the statement around 47TB of technical information about the production of Russian drones was destroyed including 10TB of backup files.

The Ukrainian Cyber Alliance said they also stole all of the source code before destroying everything and further reported:
" from the information we received, China is providing assistance in the production and training of specialists of Gaskar Group"
"The network went down so thoroughly that the doors in the building were blocked"
