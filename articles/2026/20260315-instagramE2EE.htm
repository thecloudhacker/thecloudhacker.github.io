Instagram shutting-down E2EE chat

15th March 2026

Meta has made an announcement to discontinue support for end-to-end encryption for chat on Instagram after 8th May. According to Meta, "very few people were opting in to end-to-end encrypted messaging in DMs, so we're removing this option from Instagram in the coming months.". They note that anyone wanting to continue using E2EE will have to use WhatsApp.

This change seems to go against the grain with a recent push to increase privacy, but perhaps appeases law enforcement and child safety advocates who argue the technology creates a safe space for criminals. E2EE is a huge component of privacy, especially for those living in regeimes that oppress truth and freedom of speech. The question comes, however - Does Instagram really need E2EE in it's chats? Probably not.

One might also argue - is this an easy way to force all communications through the WhatsApp platform instead? Probably so.
