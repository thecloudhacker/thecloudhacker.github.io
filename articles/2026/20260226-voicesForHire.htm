Voices For Hire

26th February 2026

The amalgamated criminal group Scattered LAPSUS$ Hunters has been discovered to offer financial incentives to recruit women to operate social engineering attacks on targets. With a successful history of utilising English-speaking women to be the voice of social engineering campaigns, it's no surprise that the group is seeking mules to do the donkey work and take the heat in exchange for some cash.

The recruitment drive, offering between $500 and $1000 depending on your success rates, may entice a range of recruits in the current financial pinch, especially with the younger generations, often themselves used for call centre work at low wages. It would not be surprising to see a flood of related arrests for those considering this to be a "safe" and quick way of making cash, only to find those pulling the strings are hidden away while you serve custodial sentances.

The social engineering scripts form part of an evolving attack process from the hybrid group. Businesses are being warned to expect polished voice impersonation with pre-written scripts.
