More n8n Woes

11th March 2026

It's been a couple of weeks, it's about time for another round of high CVE issues with n8n, the automation platform with currently more entries in my news archive than any other tech.

CVE-2026-27577 (CVSS score 9.4) and CVE-2026-27493 (CVSS score 9.5) have thankfully been patched by n8n in version 2.10.1 / 2.94 / 1.123.22, but in their unpatched state can be used together to exploit the system and exfiltrate the N8N_ENCRYPTION_KEY environment variable to decrypt every credential stored in your platform.

The Pillar Research team discovered the zero-click, unauthenticated RCE in December 2025, disclosing to the n8n team and detailing it in their blog once the responsible disclosure and patching period was completed.

I have no doubt we'll see further articles in the near future...
