Scattered Spider Convictions

16th June 2026

The two teenagers arrested for the TfL cyber attack in June, Owen Flowers and Thalha Jubair, have been sentenced to five and a half years prison term.

The pair, part of the Scattered Spider group, had been known to police for years. Flowers had been given a cease-and-decist order for minor cyber crime in October 2023 as a 16-year old. Jubair had been arrested at the age of 14 in 2021. Jubair is also wanted in the US in connection with cyber crimes against 47 US-based victims - it's not clear as yet if he will be handed-over to US law enforcement upon completion of his prison term.

Woolwich Crown Court heard that whilst in prison awaiting trial, both teenagers were discovered with contraband phones continuing to discuss and coordinate future cyber attacks.

Police also seized cryptocurrency holdings worth around one million pounds in stolen or ransomed cryptocurrency.
